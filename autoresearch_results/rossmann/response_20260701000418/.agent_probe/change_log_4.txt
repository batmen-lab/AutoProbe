Why this is expected to move the metric in the better direction:

rows right; training LGBM in log-space matches the metric's symmetric-
relative structure, so each boosting round optimizes a loss whose local
gradient lines up with how RMSPE is scored. With only 5 boosting rounds
and 8 leaves the model is still under-fit, but the loss/scale alignment
typically shaves 0.01-0.03 off val RMSPE independent of capacity. This
is a categorical/structural switch rather than a numeric knob, so it is
applied in full per the always-fully-applied rule.
