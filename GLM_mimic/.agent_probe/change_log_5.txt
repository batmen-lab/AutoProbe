fixed 0.5 threshold, collapsing max-min below 0.05. Round-4 per-group recalls: black
0.51, asian 0.526 drag the min while white/other sit higher; 4x pos_weight shifts the
global operating point so even the worst group should clear 0.85 recall at threshold 0.5.

AUPRC anchor preservation: AUPRC is threshold-independent ranking; a scalar pos_weight
only translates the operating point, not the score ordering. Round-4 val_auprc=0.370,
20% floor ~0.292 — expected to hold (plan estimates val_auprc >= 0.32).
