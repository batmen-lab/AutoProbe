Rationale: The WeightedRandomSampler from change_log_3 group-balances the
Male x target joint distribution, but at only 32 train batches/epoch each epoch
sees a thin, high-variance slice of the rare balanced groups (e.g.
Male+Heavy_Makeup), so the de-biasing signal is noisy and the metric oscillates
around 0.04 (tail_mean 0.0421 in probe_result_8, FAIL). Quadrupling to 128
batches gives the sampler far more coverage of under-represented spurious-pair
combinations per epoch, letting the model learn per-attribute visual evidence
instead of the gender proxy and stabilizing the tail below threshold. Distinct
data-quantity lever, not the LR-schedule approach that regressed in round 8.
