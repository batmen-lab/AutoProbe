Change log for round 2
======================

Plan title: Widen latent dim and hidden, drop dropout for stronger mixing

Applied edit (train.py, build_parser defaults):
  --n-latent      : 10  -> 30
  --n-hidden      : 128 -> 256
  --dropout-rate  : 0.1 -> 0.0

Other args (train-epochs, max-cells, batch-balance-strength, accelerator,
devices, seed, batch-size) left unchanged.

Rationale (per plan): n_latent=10 / n_hidden=128 / dropout=0.1 was too
low-capacity and over-regularized; per-batch kBET of 0.0/0.0 indicated
the encoder was collapsing each batch into its own region. The new values
(n_latent=30, n_hidden=256, dropout=0.0) are the canonical scVI defaults
used in the scib-metrics PBMC benchmark and give the VAE enough capacity
to align batch distributions, which is what scVI's batch-correction head
relies on. With dropout removed the latent is not forced into
batch-separating submodes.

record()/conclude() call lines: untouched.
prober.py: untouched.
user_analysis(): untouched (still empty pass-through).
ANCHOR markers and original_train_metric recording: untouched.

Discrepancies with the plan: none. The plan named three argparse defaults
and the corresponding symbols exist exactly as described in build_parser.
