A short-horizon (7-day) rmean captures recent trend shifts that neither the 28-day nor prev-28 rmeans can express, and is a well-known high-signal M5 feature. It should lower validation RMSE (direction: lower_is_better) even at the current small ensemble size. Losing `snap` is an accepted cost of the MAX_FEATURES=10 slice (touching MAX_FEATURES would be comment 2's territory and violate the one-comment rule).
